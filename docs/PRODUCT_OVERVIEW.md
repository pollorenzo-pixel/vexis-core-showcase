# Product Overview

VEXIS Core is a deployed mental performance platform created to make structured mental training easier to access and practise.

The shipped experience organises training around Foundation, Intuition, and Performance. It provides a practice library within a responsive web application, supported by user authentication and persisted user/session data.

## Intended Audience

- Everyday people who want a structured approach to mental training
- Athletes developing repeatable mental practices
- People operating in high-pressure performance environments

## Delivery Approach

The product was developed iteratively: identify a useful improvement, implement it, test it, deploy it, observe the result, and refine it. AI-assisted development accelerated exploration and implementation, while product judgement, technical choices, validation, and release decisions remained the founder's responsibility.
